Although plants may survive without the use of secondary metabolites. The extracts of these compounds have great advantages:

For example

Shikonine is a red pigment that is manufactured by Lithospermum eryrorhizon cells which has been used for cosmetics such as lipsticks.

Vincristine is manufactured by Vinca rosea and is helpful for treating cancer.

Other compounds which used to treat cardiovascular disorders include Digoxin compounds.

And Pyrithrin is a compound used as an insecticide.
